This Privacy Policy describes how ticTacToeFunStack ("we", "our", or "the App") handles information when you use our mobile application.

By installing or using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Overview

ticTacToeFunStack is an offline, two-player tic-tac-toe game. The App is designed to work without creating an account and without requiring you to submit personal information to play.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information you provide

The App does not ask you to create an account, sign in, or enter personal details such as your name, email address, phone number, or payment information.

2.2 Information collected automatically

We do not use analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, crash reporting services, or similar third-party tracking tools in the App.

The App may store game state on your device only while the App is running, such as:

  • Current board positions
  • Turn information
  • Round scores for the current session

This information is kept in memory on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. When you close the App, this session data is cleared.

2.3 Device permissions

The App requests the following permission on Android:

PermissionWhy it is included
Internet Included as part of the React Native application framework. The App does not require an internet connection to play, and we do not intentionally collect or send your personal data over the internet during normal gameplay.

The App does not request access to your contacts, camera, microphone, location, photos, files, or SMS.

3. How We Use Information

Because the App does not collect personal information from you, we do not use personal data for profiling, marketing, or advertising.

Any temporary gameplay information stays on your device for the current session so the game can function.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

We do not share your personal information with third parties for marketing or analytics purposes.

We may disclose information only if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests by public authorities.

5. Data Retention

Gameplay session data exists only while the App is open. We do not retain personal data on our servers because we do not operate user accounts or backend data collection for this App.

6. Children's Privacy

ticTacToeFunStack is suitable for general audiences. The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to design the App so that gameplay happens locally on your device. Because the App does not collect personal information on our systems, the main security consideration is the security of your own device.

No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, but we aim to keep the App simple and minimize data collection.

8. Your Choices

You can stop all App-related processing of session data at any time by closing or uninstalling the App.

If you uninstall the App, any local app data stored by the operating system may be removed according to your device settings.

9. International Users

The App is provided for use on mobile devices and does not intentionally transfer personal data internationally because it does not collect personal data for transmission to us.
