Overview ticTacToeFunStack is an **offline, two-player tic-tac-toe game**. The App is designed to work **without creating an account** and **without requiring you to submit personal information** to play. --- ## 2. Information We Collect ### 2.1 Information you provide The App does **not** ask you to create an account, sign in, or enter personal details such as your name, email address, phone number, or payment information. ### 2.2 Information collected automatically We do **not** use analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, crash reporting services, or similar third-party tracking tools in the App. The App may store **game state on your device only while the App is running**, such as: - Current board positions - Turn information - Round scores for the current session This information is kept **in memory on your device** and is **not transmitted to our servers**. When you close the App, this session data is cleared. ### 2.3 Device permissions The App requests the following permission on Android: | Permission | Why it is included | |------------|-------------------| | **Internet** | Included as part of the React Native application framework.
